# TechHub Homepage

Welcome to the **TechHub Homepage**! This project is designed as part of the TechHub website, where users can discover the latest technology deals, shop for products, and enjoy exclusive offers. The homepage features a dynamic banner and a responsive navbar.

## Table of Contents

- [Description](#description)
- [Features](#features)
- [Technologies Used](#technologies-used)
- [How to Use](#how-to-use)

## Description

The TechHub homepage includes:

- A clean and minimalist layout.
- A responsive navigation bar with links to different pages (Home, About, Contact, Login).
- A banner section showcasing the latest offers, including up to **30% off** on select items.
- A "Shop Now" button that redirects users to the homepage.

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Adjusts to various screen sizes.
- **Interactive Banner**: Features a parallax effect with promotional content.
- **Hover Effects**: Buttons and navigation links change appearance when hovered over.
- **Custom Fonts**: Utilizes simple, readable font styles for a professional look.


# Homepage

Welcome to our product website! Our homepage offers a range of features to enhance your shopping experience. Explore the various sections below to make the most out of your visit.

## Features

### Product Display
- **Browse a Diverse Range of Products**: Our homepage showcases a variety of products with detailed images, titles, prices, and optional descriptions. Find everything you need in one place.

### Product Search
- **Easily Find What You're Looking For**: Use the search bar to quickly locate products by name or keyword. Discover products efficiently without scrolling through countless pages.

### Product Filtering
- **Narrow Down Your Choices**: Filter products by categories such as electronics, clothing, and home goods. Our filtering options help you find exactly what you're interested in.

### Product Sorting
- **Organize Products Your Way**: Sort products by price (low to high, high to low) or title (ascending, descending). Customize your view to suit your preferences.

### Add to Cart
- **Convenient Shopping**: Add products to your cart with a single click. Choose your desired quantity and keep track of what you plan to buy.

### Cart Display
- **Review Your Selections**: View the contents of your cart, including product details, quantities, and total price. Make sure you’re happy with your choices before checkout.

### Checkout
- **Smooth Checkout Process**: Proceed through a simulated checkout process, entering payment information and receiving an order confirmation. Experience a streamlined and secure transaction.

## Technologies Used

- **HTML**: Provides the structure and layout of the webpage.
- **CSS**: Enhances the styling and design of the site.
- **JavaScript**: Adds dynamic functionality and interactive elements.

## Usage

- **Browse Products**: Explore the range of available products and their details right from the homepage.
- **Add to Cart**: Click the "Add to Cart" button for items you wish to purchase and select the quantity.
- **View Cart**: Access your cart by clicking the cart icon in the header to see what you’ve added.
- **Checkout**: Follow the checkout process to finalize your purchase and enter payment details.

## Customization

- **Product Data**: Update the products array in the JavaScript code to modify product information.
- **Styling**: Adjust the appearance of the site by editing the CSS styles.
- **Functionality**: Expand features with additional functionalities such as user registration, payment integration, and order history.

# Login Page

## Overview

This is a login page for TechHub. It includes a form for users to enter their email and password, along with validation for password strength and email format.

## Features

- **Navigation Bar**: Displays the TechHub logo.
- **Login Form**: Allows users to enter their email and password to log in.
- **Password Validation**: Provides real-time feedback on password strength.
- **Form Validation**: Ensures that both the email and password meet specific criteria before submission.
- **Additional Links**: Options to reset the password or sign up if the user does not have an account.

## Technologies Used

- **HTML**: Provides the structure and content of the webpage.
- **CSS**: Handles the styling and layout of the page.
- **JavaScript**: Manages form validation and real-time feedback.
